ACME TECH INC. - HUMAN RESOURCES POLICY

1. WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are Monday to Friday, 09:00 to 18:00, with a one-hour lunch break. 
Employees may request flexible working arrangements with manager approval.

2. ANNUAL LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 14 days of paid annual leave per year for the first 5 years of 
employment. After 5 years of service, this increases to 20 days per year. Unused leave 
may be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

3. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate 
is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

4. REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week, subject to manager approval and role 
requirements. Fully remote arrangements require HR and department head approval.

5. PROBATION PERIOD
New employees undergo a probation period of 3 months, during which either party may 
terminate employment with 1 week's notice.

6. TERMINATION
Employment may be terminated by either party with the following notice periods based 
on tenure: less than 6 months (2 weeks notice), 6 months to 2 years (4 weeks notice), 
more than 2 years (8 weeks notice).
